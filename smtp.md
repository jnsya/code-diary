# Email and SMTP


## Questions
- What is a protocol?


## Notes
- SMTP = Simple Mail Transfer Protocol

- *email client*
  - examples: Gmail, Outlook, etc.
  - A program that allows users to read and send emails.
  
- *email server*
  - 2 kinds of email server:
    - SMTP: outgoing mail
    - IMAP or POP3: incoming mail
